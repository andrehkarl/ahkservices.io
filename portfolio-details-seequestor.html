CASE STUDY

Accelerating Video Analytics and AI with High-Performance Media Processing

At AHK Services, we specialize in building robust, high-performance video analytics platforms that leverage the cutting edge of media processing and AI. Here's how we helped a client optimize their platform for superior capabilities and seamless operation:

We seamlessly integrated the media processing data path of their video analytics platform with the NVIDIA Deepstream SDK. This immediately unlocked advanced AI capabilities and accelerated their video analysis.

Our team implemented and integrated a high-performance frame scaling, filtering, and cropping library. This was achieved using the powerful combination of ffmpeg/libav and NVIDIA Performance Primitives (NPP), ensuring efficient and high-quality video manipulation.

We engineered individual GOP (Group of Pictures) and frame decoders, designed to intelligently utilize either GPU or CPU decoding for optimal performance based on system demands.

To guarantee reliability and rapid deployment, we implemented an automated end-to-end Continuous Integration (CI) test suite and tightly integrated it with GitLab CI/CD, ensuring every update is rigorously validated.

Our commitment extends beyond development; we provided crucial system support and troubleshooting directly at customer sites, ensuring smooth operation and rapid issue resolution.
