CASE STUDY

Bringing a Vision to Life for Expro's DHV ViewMax Camera System

At AHK Services, we thrive on transforming innovative concepts into robust, market-ready products. Here's how we partnered with Expro to evolve their DHV ViewMax Camera System:

From Prototype to Production: We took Expro's initial video capturing and storage device prototype and meticulously engineered it into a fully realized, reliable product, ready for deployment.

Unlocking Hidden Potential: Through precise reverse-engineering, we fully established the prototype's hardware and software architecture, gaining critical insights that informed its successful productization.

Building for Consistency & Quality: We implemented a rigorous, formal software build process, ensuring every development iteration of the ViewMax system was consistent, regression free, and met the highest quality standards required by the oil and gas industry.

Guaranteed Performance: We established detailed use cases specifications and derived comprehensive requirements, then leveraged these to build an automated integration test suite that rigorously validated the system's performance and functionality.

Seamless Evolution: Our experts conducted the migration of the software to the new incarnation of the ViewMax hardware platform, handling complex board bring-up activities.

Accelerated Development & Deployment: By setting up robust Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) infrastructure, we enabled faster, more reliable software releases and seamless updates for the ViewMax Camera System.
